Paint Correction

Bring your car's paint back to life with our 3 step paint correction. Vehicle is given a basic exterior wash, it then gets the claybar and gets the 3 step correction with a Dual-Action Polisher as followed:

  • Compound: Combined with a fine cutting pad the compound acts similar to sandpaper in smoothening out scratches in the paint

  • Polish: With the compound step, some of the paint's clear coat starts to haze up. The polish is combined with a polishing pad to push polish into the paint and bring back the shine

  • Wax/Protect: After this much action your paint needs something to protect it while the compound and polish get comfy in the paint. The vehicle is waxed with a paste wax to protect the paint and leave an even better shine
