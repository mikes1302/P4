About Us

Hi Welcome to JERZWALE Detailing, we are a start-up car detailing and customizing company based in Jersey City, NJ. Currently this is a one-man business run by me, Mike Singh. I started off my detailing career in high school as soon as I got my learners permit. I did not have a car but did have a love for cars and plenty of time to spend. I started off cleaning my family cars on a weekly schedule and realized that I love keeping cars clean. For some of us a car is not just a mean of transportation but rather a second home and hobby. At the age of 18 when I got my first car I kept a constant schedule to keep it up to par. Over the years of wear and tear of my car I learned new ways to maintain the longevity of my car's. I treat any car that I work on like it is my own. The satisfaction of seeing my customer's reaction when seeing their car brought back to life is a satisfaction that I could never get enough of.
